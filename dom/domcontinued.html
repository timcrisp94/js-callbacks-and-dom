Classes

The classList property offers a better approach for working with classes than the setAttributes methods. classList is an object with the following methods pertaining to classes

  • add(className, className, ...)
  • remove(className, className, ...)
  • toggle(className)
  • contains(className)
  • replace(oldClass, newClass)
